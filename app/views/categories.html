Help on this question

You can select any number of categories, provided that you meet the requirements for every category you select. For a full description and the requirements for each tier and category, please see the Tier 2 and 5 sponsor policy guidance and Tier 4 sponsor policy guidance.

If you register for Tier 2 (General) you will automatically be registered for all unrestricted sub-categories, which are:

  • New hires - high earners
  • Extensions
  • Changes of employment
  • Switching

You will also be eligible to apply for restricted certificates of sponsorship.

If you register for Tier 2 (Intra-Company Transfer) you will automatically be registered for all sub-categories, which are:

  • Long Term staff
  • Graduate Trainee

For more information on applying for a licence, please see our website: Tier 2 / 5 sponsors or Tier 4 sponsors.
