Use this service if you want to get permission to work in the UK for people from outside the European Economic Area (EEA) and Switzerland.

It is for employing people who:

  • don’t otherwise have permission to work in the UK
  • will be doing a skilled job at NQF level 6 or above

Start now

You will need

  • your national insurance number or passport number
  • the company number of the business
  • to have advertised any role in a Jobcentre Plus

There is a fee to have your application considered. For a small business it is £536 and for a large business it is £1496.

If you are granted permission, there is a fee of £250 for each person you sponsor for employment through this service.
